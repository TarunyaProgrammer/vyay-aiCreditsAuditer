# Vyay Unit Economics & Revenue Roadmap

Vyay is positioned as a high-authority top-of-funnel (ToF) engine for **Credex**, a premium AI infrastructure consultancy.

## 1. The Business Model: "Value-First Lead Gen"

Vyay operates on a **Freemium Audit** model:
- **Free**: Instant deterministic audit, shareable results, executive summary.
- **Monetization (Credex)**: High-savings audits (>$500/mo) trigger a "Strategic Consultation" CTA.
- **Affiliate Revenue**: Referral links for recommended migrations (e.g., migrating to Windsurf from Cursor).

## 2. Unit Economics (Projected)

| Metric | Target Value | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CAC (Cost per Lead)** | $2.50 | Organic growth through "Screenshot-worthy" results. |
| **Conversion Rate (Lead -> Meeting)** | 8% | For audits with savings > $500/mo. |
| **ACV (Average Contract Value)** | $5,000 | Credex infrastructure optimization retainer. |
| **LTV/CAC Ratio** | 12x | High efficiency due to viral deterministic engine. |

## 3. The $1M ARR Roadmap

### Phase 1: The Viral Audit (Months 1-3)
- Goal: 10,000 audits completed.
- Focus: Product Hunt launch, Twitter/X engineering community engagement.
- Revenue: $0 (Building authority and lead database).

### Phase 2: Enterprise Benchmarking (Months 4-8)
- Goal: 50 paid "Deep Dives" per month.
- Revenue: $25k MRR ($500 per custom audit).
- Focus: Adding SSO, team permissions, and real-time API billing integration.

### Phase 3: Credex Scaling (Months 9-12)
- Goal: $85k MRR.
- Mix: 60% consultancy retainers, 20% premium audits, 20% affiliate/referral revenue.
