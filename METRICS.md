# Vyay Success Metrics & Tracking

We track performance across three pillars: **Product Health, Growth Viralilty, and Lead Quality**.

## 1. Product Health (Retention & Value)
- **Audit Completion Rate**: % of users who start an audit and reach the Result Page. (Target: >85%)
- **Average Time-to-Value**: Seconds from landing to first savings recommendation. (Target: < 90s)
- **Data Accuracy**: Manual verification of $100 \%$ of `PRICING_DATA.md` against vendor sites every 30 days.

## 2. Growth Virality (The Sharing Loop)
- **Share Ratio**: Number of "Result Shares" (X/LinkedIn) per 100 audits. (Target: 5%)
- **Organic Lift**: % of new visitors coming from a shareable `public_id` URL. (Target: 20%)
- **K-Factor**: The number of new users generated by each existing user. (Target: 0.15 for early stage)

## 3. Lead Quality (Credex Pipeline)
- **Lead Capture Rate**: % of users who submit email/company after the audit. (Target: 12%)
- **High-Impact Conversion**: % of users with >$500/mo savings who click "Schedule Strategic Call". (Target: 15%)
- **Referral Efficiency**: Number of leads generated per active referral code. (Target: 2.5)

## 4. Technical Performance
- **Gemini Latency**: Response time for AI Summary generation. (Target: < 2.5s)
- **Fallback Trigger Rate**: % of audits using the deterministic summary fallback. (Target: < 2%)
- **Lighthouse Performance**: Global performance score on mobile. (Target: > 95)
