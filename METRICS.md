# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) — Project Vyay

Organizational focus is directed toward "Value-Optimization" metrics.

## Primary Strategic Metric
- **Aggregate Identified Savings (USD)**: The cumulative volume of capital identified as sub-optimally allocated across all system evaluations.

## Operational and Engagement Metrics
1. **Evaluation Completion Rate**: Percentage of users who successfully navigate from the initial data ingestion to the final reporting phase.
2. **Time-to-Value (TTV)**: Mean duration from landing interface engagement to the presentation of initial recommendations. (Target: < 60 seconds).
3. **Virality Coefficient (K-Factor)**: Mean number of secondary evaluations generated per instance of professional report distribution.
4. **Service Density per Evaluation**: Mean quantity of service entities analyzed per evaluation cycle, serving as an indicator of organizational infrastructure complexity.

## Technical Performance Metrics
1. **Analytical Latency**: P99 response duration for AI-assisted analytical processing.
2. **System Error Incidence**: Percentage of unsuccessful evaluation calculations relative to total requests.
3. **Interface Responsiveness**: Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) performance for the landing interface.
